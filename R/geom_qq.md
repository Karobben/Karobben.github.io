## QQ plot
```r
ggplot(mtcars,aes(sample=mpg)) + geom_qq(aes(color='qq')) + geom_point(aes(mpg,cyl,color='point'))+
	geom_qq_line(aes(color='qqline'))+ theme_light()
```
